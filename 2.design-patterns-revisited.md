# Design Patterns Revisited
[Contents](./readme.md#contents)

*Design Patterns: Elements of Reusable Object-Oriented Software* is over twenty years old. For an industry as quickly moving as software, that's practically ancient. The enduring popularity of the book says something about how timeless design is compared to many frameworks and methodologies.

While *Design Patterns* is still relevant, we've learned a lot in the past couple of decades. In this section, we'll walk through a handful of the original patterns the Gang of Four documented. For each pattern, I hope to have something useful or interesting to say.
